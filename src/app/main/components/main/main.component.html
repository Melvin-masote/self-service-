Who can claim?
All benefits are payable to the policy owner:
  • If the policyholder is the deceased then the benefit is payable
    to the beneficiary of the death benefit.
  • If there is no beneficiary appointed then the benefit is payable
    to the nominee for ownership.
  • If there is no beneficiary or nominee then the benefit is payable
    to the next insured as indicated in the policy contract.
What we will do within 48 hours after receiving the claim?
  • Inform the person claiming if we need any other information.
  • Inform the person claiming about our decision to either approve or decline the claim
